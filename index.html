About

High-skilled immigrants, migrant founders, foreign-born entrepreneurs...whatever the label, these people are outliers: they face above-average challenges in the capital markets but have created more than half of the US unicorns.

We believe that cross-border entrepreneurship creates value for “both sides of the Pond”.

We're proud to be part of this community and work every day to make it easier to build & launch for the fellow founders that chose the US as their global launchpad.

We invest at pre-seed & seed in tech companies like Loadex and Interstellar Lab.

If you want to discuss investment opportunities, fill out the form. If you'd like to talk, let’s connect.
